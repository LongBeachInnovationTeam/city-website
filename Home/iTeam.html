LONG BEACH INNOVATION TEAM

The Innovation Team (i-team) was launched in May of 2015 by Mayor Robert Garcia and City Manager Pat West to collaboratively create sustainable solutions for the city's greatest challenges.

The i-team extends the City's capacity to rapidly advance research and development through a bold, data-driven, and transparent approach to innovation. Co-created solutions will improve the lives of all who call Long Beach their home.

The team is generously supported by Long Beach City Council and Bloomberg Philanthropies.

The i-team is currently focused on identifying economic development opportunities that will stimulate new investment, create jobs, strengthen our workforce, and improve the city's business friendliness. Learn more about our current challenge, Economic Development.

The City of Long Beach has partnered with Citymart, with the support of the Long Beach Community Foundation and the Knight Foundation, to engage you in a creative, problem-solving approach that can change how the city foundations and performs. Citymart is a New York-based company that transforms the way cities solve problems, connecting them with new ideas through open challenges to entrepreneurs and citizens.

The City will issue (5) five service challanges. Over the next 18 months Citymart will play a consulting role, helping City staff and community stakeholders define the challanges and envision outcomes.

Challenge 1: Launch, Do, Grow Long Beach

The City of Long Beach is looking for better ways to support entrepreneurs launching, operating and growing their business. First time entrepreneurs need accurate information on the steps of starting and operating a business, as well as proactive assistance navigating government along the way.
